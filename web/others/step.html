Sharing Memories
Instruction
You will have the following 4 steps to share your story.
Write your story and location address on the postcard next to the mailbox.
Make the side with words upper and put the postcard into the mailbox. The content will be recorded by a camera.
The content of the postcard will be shared with others through the internet, and others can reply you with pictures and comments.
You will find your name at the upper right corner if you get any feedbacks. And you can watch all your feedbacks in a slideshow.
